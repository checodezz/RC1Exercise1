Notable Awards and Honors

  • National Medal of Technology and Innovation - for his role in the creation of the personal computer (1992).
  • Albert A. Michelson Medal - for outstanding contributions to physics, awarded by The Franklin Institute (2002).
  • Honorary Knighthood - by Queen Elizabeth II for contributions to enterprise (2005).
  • Time Person of the Year - alongside Melinda Gates and Bono, for influence in business and technology (2005).
  • Prince of Asturias Award for International Cooperation - for philanthropic work (2006).
  • Bower Award for Business Leadership - by The Franklin Institute (2010).
  • Padma Bhushan (India) - one of India's highest civilian awards, for philanthropy (2015).
  • Presidential Medal of Freedom - awarded by President Barack Obama for philanthropic work (2016).
  • Knight Commander of the Order of the British Empire (KBE) - an honorary knighthood by Queen Elizabeth II for services to global health and poverty reduction (2009).
  • Axel Springer Award - for contributions to business, technology, and social initiatives (2018).
  • Global Citizen Prize for Business Leader - for dedication to global health and climate change initiatives (2020).
  • Dan David Prize - for impact on technology and philanthropy (2021).
