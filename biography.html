Birth

October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington, USA.

Co-Founder of Microsoft

Founded Microsoft in 1975 with Paul Allen, playing a pivotal role in the personal computer revolution.

Microsoft Success

Led Microsoft to become a global technology giant with products like MS-DOS, Windows, and Microsoft Office.

Philanthropy

Established the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation in 2000, focusing on global health, education, and poverty alleviation.

Author

Wrote "The Road Ahead" in 1995, outlining his vision for the future of technology.

Wealth

Consistently ranked among the world's wealthiest individuals.

Global Vision

Advocated for solutions to global challenges, including climate change, healthcare, and education.
